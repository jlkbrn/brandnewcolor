About

Bridging creativity
and technology.

With over 20 years at the intersection of technology and visual storytelling, Marek Brandys brings technical precision and artistic sensitivity to every project.

From digital intermediate workflows for theatrical releases to premium streaming content, he partners with directors and cinematographers to realise their visual vision — ensuring every frame communicates with intention.

Based in Warsaw, Marek works across feature films, documentary series, entertainment formats, commercials and broadcast television. His technical background in mechatronics and postgraduate studies in graphic design give him a unique perspective: rigorous in process, refined in aesthetic.
